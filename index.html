A tool to keep up with the Internet's trends

MetaTrend is a platform that collects trend data from prominent social networks, and delivers it to your fingertips. It is intuitive and easy to use, and is perfect for individuals and businesses alike.

Who can use MetaTrend?

Here's who we can think of, but surely people will surprise us.

Marketers

An overview of the entire Internet. Deploy campaigns effectively, and easily cater to the masses.

Netizens

A place to keep up with the ever-changing digital world. Never miss out on current events again.

Analysts

Statistics, infographics, and records, all in one easy to access place.

Advanced search algorithms to deliver you accurate, detailed information

MetaTrend's search AI aggregates trend data from large social networks such as Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and Reddit. It then compiles this data in a way that is easy to navigate and understand.

All the infographics you'll ever need

MetaTrend's front page displays all the essential trend data using a wide variety of modules. This includes graphs, charts, numbers, links, word clouds, and even a personalized RSS feed.

Intuitive and customizable user interface

MetaTrend's user interface is based off of modules. Each module has drag and drop functionality, and many can be expanded and collapsed to show more or less information. Every aspect of MetaTrend's interface can also be customized to suit your needs, and modules can be enabled or disabled. You can even create different pages with their own separate modules!
